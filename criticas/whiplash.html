Sometimes, some problems that happened in the past from the last review I wrote remain the same. One of these issues is my own weight. My weight has been bothering me for many years. I was always very thin, man. When I was a child, I was always thin. I had a normal weight for my age. Until 2019, I was at my peak, weighing between 70 and 75 kilos. But since the pandemic, that changed drastically, and I'm a very anxious person. Staying still and being at home was the worst thing that could have happened.

So, "Whiplash" is my peace. I thought, damn. But it's a movie that gives me the perspective that everything will be okay, you know.

I actually watched the first hour alone. Then my dad joined me. He always has wonderful comments when he watches a movie with me. I love him so much; he says some gems. But my dad is very funny. This movie will really stay as my favorite movie for many years. Since I watched "Whiplash" for the first time in 2018, it changed who I am. And, man, I think that's it. I love this movie passionately. I had a lot of emotions. I love you so much, Miles Teller. I love you so much, J.K. Simmons. You delivered one of the most brilliant performances I've ever seen.
